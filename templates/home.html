How it works

The xray is analyzed by a pretrained convolutional neural network trained on a combination of these datasets: NIH, RSNA, CheXpert, Mimic-CXR and PadChest. The AI then runs the image through a neural network which predicts the probabilities of each disease. Contributing & Deploying

Welcome to our project! This website showcases the potential of AI systems in analyzing X-rays, demonstrating that with time and rigorous training, AI can significantly aid in triaging patients. Our goal is to provide a cost-effective triage solution for hospitals across India, especially in areas where specialized medical staff might be scarce. By leveraging AI, we aim to streamline the triage process, improving efficiency and accuracy without the need for additional staff. This initiative is designed to support healthcare facilities in delivering timely and effective care, ultimately benefiting patients and the healthcare system as a whole. This project is created by Prakhar Biswas, who is passionate about advancing healthcare through innovative technology. We believe that with the continued development of AI technologies, we can make a meaningful impact on healthcare accessibility and quality. Thank you for joining us on this journey to enhance medical triage through cutting-edge AI solutions.

FAQ's

Nope, this project does not aim to completely replace doctors. It is just a mere companion to assist doctors in diagnosing and treating a patient.

The average human radiologist have error rates of upto 5%. With sufficient data supplied into the AI's dataset, it can theoretically reach and surpass a human's accuracy. Even if it does not reach such high accuracy levels, it can still be used to aid humans.

This project can diagnose the following: Atelectasis, Consolidation, Inflitration, Pneumothorax, Edema, Emphysema, Fibrosis, Effusion, Pneumonia, Pleural Thickening, Cardiomegaly, Nodule, Mass, Hernia, Lung Lesion, Fractures, Lung Opacity and Enlarged Cardiomediastinum.

Currently, this app aims to assist healthcare workers only, however, the code can be tweaked to allow patients to view the results which has been analysed by their doctors.

The future of this app involves expanding its AI capabilities to enhance accuracy and efficiency in triaging X-rays, and scaling its deployment to support more hospitals across India. Long-term, it aims to integrate seamlessly into healthcare systems, improving patient care and accessibility.
